About

A Home with a Past, Built for the Present

Tucked into a quiet street in historic Texarkana, The Pecan House is a turn-of-the-century Victorian given new life through a careful, room-by-room restoration. Original hardwood floors, soaring ceilings, and restored architectural details share space with thoughtful modern updates — two full kitchens, six refreshed bathrooms, and the kind of beds that make you want to stay an extra night.

Designed for groups who appreciate the difference between a place to stay and a place worth remembering. Whether you're hosting a family reunion, a milestone celebration, or a long weekend with friends, the house gives you room to spread out and reasons to stay in.

The renovation direction pairs restored historic detail with deep green rooms, warm pecan wood, antique brass, crisp bedding, marble baths, and custom Pecan House hospitality touches. Renderings and design mockups will be replaced with final photography when renovation is complete.

Good to Know

The small-print answers, before you ask.

When is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Self check-in via smart lock — your code arrives the morning of your stay.

How many guests can the house sleep?

The main house sleeps up to 12 across six bedrooms. The carriage house apartment sleeps up to 4 and is listed separately for smaller groups.

Is parking available?

Yes — on-site parking for up to three vehicles, with additional off-street options nearby.

Are pets allowed?

We're a pet-free property to keep the home comfortable for all guests, including those with allergies. Service animals are welcome in accordance with applicable law.

Can the carriage house apartment be booked separately?

Yes. The carriage house has its own listing on Airbnb and VRBO and sleeps up to 4 — perfect for couples, small families, or solo travelers who want a private space.

Do you support corporate bookings or custom rates?

Yes. We support corporate housing, professional stays, extended stays, and custom rates. Use the corporate inquiry form or join the waitlist to share your company, dates, guest count, and booking needs.
